VR Exposure Therapy Project

Exploring immersive technology to aid mental health treatment

Project Description

Our project focuses on creating a virtual reality (VR) experience designed for exposure therapy. The goal is to provide a safe, controlled environment where individuals can gradually confront and overcome their fears or anxieties using immersive simulation.

This initiative combines psychology, technology, and design to make therapeutic practices more accessible and effective.
